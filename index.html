About Me

I’m a UI designer with a background in Game Design, where I focused on level design and in-game UI. Designing for players taught me how to guide attention, reduce friction, and make complex systems feel approachable.

I apply those same principles to interface design using layout, contrast, and spacing to lead users naturally through an experience. My goal is to create interfaces that feel intuitive on first use and satisfying over time.
